Why do I need a VPN?
There are 2 main reasons for using a VPN: to protect your online information and to visit websites that can be hard to enjoy locally.

1. Protect Yourself: When left unprotected, your private data, such as bank account information and credit card numbers, can fall into the wrong hands. A good VPN encrypts your data, so even if you connect to a public wi-fi network, your private data is guaranteed to be protected.

2. Watch and Browse How You Want: Website availability is often tailored to where you live, whether it’s by the local government or a streaming site like Netflix and Hulu. The VPN helps you pick a new location to get that local experience, like being in a virtual New York City. This VPN for example shows more than 500 TV channels and always help you watch like a local. .
Is it legal to use a VPN?
In most countries there is nothing illegal about having and using a VPN.

There are illegal activities, such as downloading copyrighted material, that can be done with a VPN.

But the VPN itself is legal and as we mentioned above has plenty of great reasons for installing it on your computer.
Will using a VPN affect my internet speed?
It is possible for there to be a slight slowdown in your internet speed when connected to a server that is overburdened. You can fix this by simply choosing another server to connect to.

There is also the possibility that you'll experience faster speeds than you're used to. Many ISPs have been known to throttle, or slow down, your speed based on your online activities. By using a VPN you will hide your internet activities from the ISP and avoid having your speed throttled.

For ultra-fast speeds get ExpressVPN, which is the fastest VPN we tested, thanks to their unlimited bandwidth and over 1,000 servers.
Can I setup a VPN on my smartphone?
Of course.

Before purchasing a VPN, double check to make sure that they offer a downloadable app, and then safely browse from your phone or tablet on any Wi-Fi without worry. Some VPNs will allow multiple devices to connect simultaneously, which means you can connect your PC, laptop, phone, router, and wife’s phone all for one low price.
